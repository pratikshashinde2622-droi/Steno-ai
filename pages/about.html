About This Project

Online Examination System

A modern, browser-based examination portal built for students and faculty of Arts, Commerce & Science College, Sonai — affiliated with Savitribai Phule Pune University.

What Is This?
A Digital Exam Experience

This Online Examination System allows students to register, log in, attempt timed MCQ-based exams, and instantly see their results — all from a web browser, with no installation required. Administrators can manage students, create exams and questions, and view results from a dedicated dashboard.

🎓

For Students

Register with your roll number, attempt available exams, track your scores, and view past results anytime.

👨‍💼

For Administrators

Add and manage students, create exams with custom durations, build question banks, and monitor results.

⏱️

Timed Exams

Every exam runs with a live countdown timer. When time expires, your answers are submitted automatically.

📊

Instant Results

Results are calculated immediately after submission showing marks, percentage, and pass/fail status.

Features
What You Can Do

Everything you need for a complete online examination experience — built right into the browser.

🔐
Secure Authentication

Separate logins for students (roll number + password) and admin (credentials-based).

📝
Student Self-Registration

Students can register themselves with roll number, name, email, contact, and password.

📋
MCQ-Based Exams

Four-option multiple choice questions with customizable marks per question.

⏰
Auto-Submit Timer

Exam timer counts down and auto-submits when time runs out — no cheating gaps.

📈
Result Analytics

Admin can view all student results with marks, percentage, and pass/fail status in a table.

💾
Persistent Data

All data is stored in the browser's localStorage — no server or database needed.

📱
Fully Responsive

Works seamlessly on desktops, tablets, and mobile phones.

🧩
Multi-Exam Support

Create multiple exams across different subjects. Students see only exams with questions available.
